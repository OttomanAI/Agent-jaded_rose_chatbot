# Jaded Rose — Returns & Exchange Policy

## Overview

We want you to love everything you order from Jaded Rose. If something is not quite right, we are happy to accept returns and exchanges within **30 days** of the delivery date.

## Eligibility

To be eligible for a return or exchange, items must be:

- **Unworn and unwashed** — tried on is fine, but the item must not have been worn out.
- **In original condition** — no marks, stains, odours, pet hair or damage.
- **With all original tags attached** — swing tags and hygiene stickers must still be in place.
- **In original packaging** — items should be returned in the bag or box they arrived in.

### Items that cannot be returned

The following items are **final sale** and cannot be returned or exchanged:

- Underwear, swimwear and bodysuits (for hygiene reasons)
- Pierced jewellery
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or purchased with a discount greater than 50%
- Gift cards

## How to Initiate a Return

1. **Contact us** — Email support@jadedrose.com or message us on live chat / WhatsApp with your order number and the item(s) you wish to return.
2. **Receive your returns reference** — We will send you a unique returns reference number (e.g. RET-A1B2C3) and a prepaid returns label (UK only).
3. **Pack your item** — Place the item in its original packaging, include the returns reference inside the parcel.
4. **Post your return** — Drop the parcel at any Royal Mail collection point or Post Office using the prepaid label.

## Refunds

- Refunds are processed within **5–10 business days** of us receiving your return at our warehouse.
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**.
- If you paid with Klarna or Clearpay, your remaining instalments will be adjusted automatically.
- **Shipping costs are non-refundable** unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, faulty product).

## Exchanges

- We offer **free exchanges** on all UK orders.
- When initiating your return, let us know the new size or colour you would like and we will ship it as soon as we receive your original item.
- If the requested size or colour is out of stock, we will issue a full refund instead.

## Faulty or Incorrect Items

If you receive a **faulty, damaged or incorrect item**, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with:

- Your order number
- A photo of the fault or issue
- A description of the problem

We will arrange a **free return** and send a replacement or full refund — whichever you prefer.

## International Returns

- International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.
- We recommend using a tracked service as we cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit.
- Customs duties or taxes paid on the original order are non-refundable — please check with your local customs authority.

## Late Returns

Returns received after the 30-day window will not be accepted. We will contact you to arrange return of the item at your cost, or you may request store credit as an alternative.
