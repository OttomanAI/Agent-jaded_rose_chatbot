# Jaded Rose — Frequently Asked Questions

## About Jaded Rose

**What is Jaded Rose?**
Jaded Rose is a UK-based online clothing brand specialising in contemporary womenswear and menswear. We design trend-led pieces that are accessible, sustainable and made to last. Our collections drop seasonally and we regularly release limited-edition capsules.

**Where are you based?**
Our headquarters and warehouse are in Manchester, UK. We ship across the United Kingdom and internationally to over 30 countries.

## Delivery & Shipping

**How long does delivery take?**
- **UK Standard:** 3–5 working days (free on orders over £50)
- **UK Express:** 1–2 working days (£5.99)
- **UK Next Day:** Order before 2 PM for next working day delivery (£7.99)
- **Europe:** 5–10 working days (£9.99)
- **Rest of World:** 7–14 working days (£14.99)

**Do you offer free shipping?**
Yes! All UK orders over £50 qualify for free standard delivery. We also run free-shipping promotions during seasonal sales.

**Do you ship internationally?**
Yes, we ship to over 30 countries including the US, Canada, Australia, and most of Europe. International customers may be subject to local customs duties and taxes which are the responsibility of the recipient.

**Can I track my order?**
Absolutely. Once your order has been dispatched you will receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your parcel in real time.

## Payment

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Klarna (Pay in 3), and Clearpay.

**Do you offer Buy Now Pay Later?**
Yes, we offer Klarna Pay in 3 and Clearpay on all orders. Simply select your preferred option at checkout — no interest, no fees if you pay on time.

**Are your prices inclusive of VAT?**
Yes, all prices displayed on our website include UK VAT at 20%.

## Discount Codes & Promotions

**How do I use a discount code?**
Enter your code in the "Discount code" box at checkout and click "Apply". Only one code can be used per order. Discount codes cannot be combined with sale items unless stated otherwise.

**Do you do student discount?**
Yes! We offer 15% off for verified students through Student Beans. Sign up with your university email to get your code.

**When is your next sale?**
We run seasonal sales (end of season, Black Friday, Boxing Day) and flash sales throughout the year. Sign up to our newsletter or follow us on Instagram @jadedrose for early access.

## Sizing

**How do I find my size?**
Check our size guide on each product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size chart. If you are between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a comfortable fit.

**Do your clothes run true to size?**
Our sizing is based on standard UK sizing. Individual product pages include fit notes — look for "true to size", "relaxed fit", or "fitted" in the description.

**What if my item doesn't fit?**
No worries — we offer free exchanges on all UK orders. Simply initiate a return and select "exchange" as your reason. We will ship your new size free of charge.

## Care Instructions

**How should I care for my clothes?**
Each garment includes a care label with specific washing instructions. As a general guide:
- Wash at 30°C or cold to preserve colour and shape
- Turn inside out before washing
- Avoid tumble drying — hang or lay flat to dry
- Iron on low heat, inside out
- Store knitwear folded, not hung, to avoid stretching

## Account & Orders

**Do I need an account to place an order?**
No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating an account lets you track orders, save addresses, and access exclusive member perks.

**Can I change or cancel my order?**
We process orders quickly, so changes or cancellations must be requested within 1 hour of placing the order. Contact us at support@jadedrose.com and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

**I haven't received my confirmation email.**
Check your spam or junk folder first. If it is not there, contact us with your name and order details and we will resend it.
