### Women's — How to Measure

- **Bust:** Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level.
- **Waist:** Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist (usually just above the belly button).
- **Hips:** Measure around the widest part of your hips, approximately 20 cm below your waist.

### Women's — Fit Tips

- **Relaxed fit / Oversized:** If you prefer a looser look, your regular size will work — no need to size up.
- **Fitted / Bodycon:** If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort.
- **Denim / Trousers:** Jaded Rose denim uses UK sizing.  If you usually wear a 28-inch waist, that corresponds to approximately a UK 10.

### Men's — How to Measure

- **Chest:** Measure around the widest part of your chest, under your arms.
- **Waist:** Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape comfortably loose.
- **Hips:** Measure around the widest point of your hips.

### Men's — Fit Tips

- **Regular fit:** Our default cut — room to move without being baggy.
- **Slim fit:** A closer cut through the body.  If you are between sizes, go one size up.
- **Oversized:** Designed to be roomy.  Order your usual size for the intended oversized look.
